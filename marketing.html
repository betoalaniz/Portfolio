Affiliate Marketing

Affiliate marketing is a performance-based marketing strategy where businesses reward affiliates for driving traffic or sales to their products or services. Affiliates earn a commission for each sale or action generated through their promotional efforts, making it a popular and cost-effective way for companies to expand their reach and increase sales.
